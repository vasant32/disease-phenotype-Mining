The case of a 33-year-old woman suffering from the Klippel-Trenaunay-syndrome which belongs to the syndromes of dysplasia is discussed. The cases in question is an anaeviformous variant. Apart from this there exists a renal hypoplasia which is to be seen within the Klippel-Trenaunay-syndrome. This renal hypoplasia has led to a terminal renal insufficiency and has made necessary a chronic haemodialytic treatment.
