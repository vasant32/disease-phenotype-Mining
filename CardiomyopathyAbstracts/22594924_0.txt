We report the first case of proliferative retinopathy associated with Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD) treated with intra-vitreal bevacizumab. The patient is a twenty-three year old quadriplegic male with ventilatory failure and cardiomyopathy requiring intermittent bilevel positive airway pressure (BiPAP ®) for the past twelve months. The patient had complained of two weeks of loss of vision in the right eye associated with pain and photophobia © 2012 The Authors.
