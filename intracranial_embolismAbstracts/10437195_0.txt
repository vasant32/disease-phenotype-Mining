Forty-two cases of apoplectic hemiplegia treated by digital acupoint pressure (DAP) therapy are briefly reported in this paper. This results were: basic cure in 10 cases (23.8%), marked effect in 17 cases (40.5%) and effect in 11 cases (26.2%). The total effective rate reached 90.5%. This therapy has several advantages, such as simple and convenient operation, low cost, reliable results and so on.
