Prevalence of different forms of acute cerebral accidents (ACA) and factors, provoking their development, has been investigated in the population of Kishinev. Annual ACA morbidity made up 226 per 100000. More than 80% of ACA patients had brain infarction (BI), 13.5%--intracerebral and 6.5% subarachnoidal hemorrhage. High BI risk has been established in patients with arterial hypertension, heart failure, elevated blood levels of sugar, cholesterol, beta-lipoprotein as well as with increased blood viscosity.
