A 12-year-old boy acquired two days after an accident with a light brain-commotion an acute tonsillitis, followed by a leftside peritonsillitis, infection of the spatium parapharyngeum, ascending to the endocranium with a thrombosis of the sinus cavernosus and septicopyaemia. Complete healing by Antibiotics, Heparin and blood-transfusions. In an judgment for an insurrance company the author states that the accident and commotion partly (30%) was the cause for the tonsillar complications.
