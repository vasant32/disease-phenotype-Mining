The autogenous saphenous vein is the preferred conduit for repair of occluded arteries in the limb and coronary arteries. The aneurysmal dilatation of the vein graft is a unusual complication in the long-term period. A case of aneurysmal formation of the vein graft for the subclavian aneurysm 5 years after grafting with classic Beh√ßet disease was reported. The etiology of this rare complication and the special surgical problem were also discussed.
