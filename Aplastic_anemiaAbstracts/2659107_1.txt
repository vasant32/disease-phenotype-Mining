The International Bone Marrow Transplant Registry (IBMTR) receives and analyses detailed information contributed by transplant teams at more than 175 institutions worldwide. This collaborative research effort has grown rapidly; there are now more than 8000 cases in the database. This is a summary of the current status of bone marrow transplantation in leukemia and severe aplastic anemia, a brief summary of key findings reported by the IBMTR during the past year, as well as studies planned for the coming year.
