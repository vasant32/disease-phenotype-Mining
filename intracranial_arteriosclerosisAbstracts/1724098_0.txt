Hemorheological Treatment in Vertigo. Depending on data of hemorheology disturbances in risk factors (1.2.3.) and carotid artery arterosclerosis--progression in vertigo of non-vestibular origin (4.) this study evaluates treatment effects by basis, i.e. correction of risk factors only, versus additional hemorheological treatment (Lowering hct., HAES, Pentoxifylline) Patients: N = 88 fe. 51 m 37 age 25-86 mean 65.1.
